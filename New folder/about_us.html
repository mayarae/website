About Us

Anchor is a community restaurant commited to only serving the best seafood to our costomers. All of our meals only use organic and family farmed ingredients because we want the best for you and the environment. All of our fruits and veggetables are grown by farmers in the Okanagan Valley and all of our fish comes from the Pacific ocean on the coast of Vancover.
